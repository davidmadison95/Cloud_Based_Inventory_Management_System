Key Features

Real-time Tracking

Monitor your inventory, orders, and shipments in real-time, ensuring you always have up-to-date information. Our system provides instant updates, allowing you to make informed decisions quickly.

  • Live inventory count updates
  • Real-time order status tracking
  • Instant shipment location updates

User Authentication

Secure access to your inventory data with robust user authentication systems. Control who has access to sensitive information and maintain data integrity with role-based permissions.

  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Role-based access control
  • Audit logs for all user actions

Automated Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports automatically, reducing manual tracking by 20%. Get insights into your inventory trends, sales patterns, and more with just a few clicks.

  • Customizable report templates
  • Scheduled report generation
  • Export options (PDF, CSV, Excel)

Low Stock Alerts

Receive timely alerts for low stock levels, preventing stockouts and optimizing your inventory. Set custom thresholds and get notified via email or SMS when it's time to reorder.

  • Customizable alert thresholds
  • Multi-channel notifications
  • Automated reorder suggestions
